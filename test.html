Fractals

Controls

  • Click on the Mandelbrot set to generate the Julia set that corresponds with the complex number
  • Click on the 'Zoom In...' button and then click on the Mandelbrot set to draw a rectangle to zoom in on
  • Drag the Mandelbrot set to pan the image
  • Click on the 'Reset Image' button to return to the starting view of the Mandelbrot set.
